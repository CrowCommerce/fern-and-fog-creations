## Managing Products (Shopify)

**What You Need:** Shopify Admin access to your store

**Access:** https://[your-store].myshopify.com/admin

### Adding a New Product

**Step 1: Navigate to Products**
1. Log into Shopify Admin
2. Click "Products" in left sidebar
3. Click "Add product" button

**Step 2: Enter Product Details**

**Required Fields:**
- **Title:** Product name (e.g., "Ocean Blue Sea Glass Earrings")
- **Description:** Full product story and details
  - Materials used
  - Inspiration/story
  - Size information
  - Care instructions
- **Media:** Upload product photos
  - Minimum 1 image required
  - Recommended 3-5 images from different angles
  - JPG or PNG, under 2MB each

**Optional Fields:**
- **Pricing:**
  - Price: Regular selling price
  - Compare at price: Original price (for showing discounts)
- **Inventory:**
  - SKU: Internal product code
  - Track quantity: Enable if you want inventory tracking
  - Quantity: How many in stock
- **Shipping:**
  - Weight: For shipping calculations
  - Requires shipping: Usually checked
- **Variants:**
  - Click "Add variant" if product has options (colors, sizes, etc.)
  - Add options like: Color (Blue, Green), Size (Small, Large)
  - Set price/SKU/inventory per variant

**Step 3: Organize Product**

**Collections:**
- Scroll to "Product organization" section
- Click "Add to collection"
- Select existing collection (e.g., "Earrings", "Sea Glass", "Best Sellers")
- Or create new collection

**Tags:**
- Add searchable tags (e.g., "sea-glass", "sterling-silver", "blue")
- Use lowercase with hyphens
- Helps with filtering and search

**Step 4: Set Product Status**

- **Active:** Product is live and purchasable
- **Draft:** Product is hidden, not yet for sale
- **Archived:** Product is removed from store

**Step 5: Save**
- Click "Save" button (top right)
- Product is now live on your website (if Active)

**Time Required:** 10-15 minutes per product

---

### Editing an Existing Product

**Step 1: Find Product**
1. Go to Shopify Admin → Products
2. Use search bar or browse list
3. Click product to edit

**Step 2: Make Changes**
- Update any field (title, description, price, images)
- Changes are highlighted in yellow
- Add/remove/reorder images

**Step 3: Save**
- Click "Save" button
- Changes appear on website within 1-2 minutes

---

### Managing Product Images

**Best Practices:**

**Image Requirements:**
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Size:** 1200x1200px minimum, 2400x2400px ideal
- **Aspect Ratio:** Square (1:1) recommended
- **File Size:** Under 2MB each
- **Background:** White or natural setting

**Image Order:**
- First image = main product photo (shows in listings)
- Additional images = detail shots, lifestyle photos, size reference
- Drag and drop to reorder

**Editing Images:**
1. Click product to edit
2. Scroll to "Media" section
3. To add: Click "Add media" → Upload files
4. To remove: Hover over image → Click trash icon
5. To reorder: Drag and drop images
6. Click "Save"

---

### Managing Product Variants

**When to Use Variants:**
- Product comes in multiple colors
- Product comes in multiple sizes
- Product has different material options

**Example:** Sea glass earrings in Blue, Green, and Amber

**Adding Variants:**

1. Edit product
2. Scroll to "Variants" section
3. Click "Add variant" or "Add options"
4. **Add Option:**
   - Option name: "Color"
   - Option values: "Blue", "Green", "Amber"
5. Shopify creates 3 variants automatically
6. **For Each Variant:**
   - Set individual price (if different)
   - Set SKU (e.g., "EAR-SG-BLU")
   - Set inventory quantity
   - Upload variant-specific image (optional)
7. Click "Save"

**Editing Variant Details:**
1. Click variant in list
2. Update price, SKU, quantity, etc.
3. Click "Save"

**Deleting a Variant:**
1. Find variant in list
2. Click three dots → "Delete variant"
3. Confirm deletion
4. Click "Save"

---

### Managing Inventory

**Tracking Inventory:**

1. Edit product
2. Scroll to "Inventory" section
3. Check "Track quantity"
4. Enter quantity on hand
5. **Options:**
   - "Continue selling when out of stock" (allow backorders)
   - "This product has a SKU or barcode"
6. Click "Save"

**Updating Stock Levels:**

**Method 1: Edit Product**
1. Go to Products → Click product
2. Update "Quantity" field
3. Click "Save"

**Method 2: Inventory Page**
1. Go to "Inventory" in sidebar
2. See all products and stock levels
3. Click quantity to edit
4. Enter new quantity
5. Saves automatically

**Low Stock Alerts:**
- Shopify can email you when stock is low
- Go to Settings → Notifications
- Enable "Low stock" notification

---

### Archiving or Deleting Products

**Archiving (Recommended):**
- Removes product from store
- Preserves product data and order history
- Can be reactivated later

**How to Archive:**
1. Edit product
2. Change status to "Archived"
3. Click "Save"

**Deleting (Permanent):**
- Completely removes product
- Cannot be undone
- Use only if product was created in error

**How to Delete:**
1. Edit product
2. Click "Delete product" (bottom of page)
3. Confirm deletion

## Creating Marketing Pages (Builder.io)

**What You Need:** Builder.io access to Fern & Fog workspace

**Access:** https://builder.io/content

### Creating a New Page

**Step 1: Start New Page**
1. Log into Builder.io
2. Click "Content" in left sidebar
3. Click "+ New" button
4. Select "page" model

**Step 2: Configure Page**
- **Name:** Internal reference (e.g., "Summer Collection Landing")
- **URL Path:** Web address (e.g., `/summer-collection`)
  - Must start with `/`
  - Use lowercase and hyphens
  - Example: `/about-us`, `/blog/sea-glass-101`

**Step 3: Build Page Content**
1. Click "Edit" to open visual editor
2. Drag components from left sidebar onto canvas
3. Click component to configure properties
4. Fill in text, images, links in right panel

**Step 4: Preview**
1. Click "Preview" button (top right)
2. Test on desktop and mobile
3. Click all links to verify
4. Go back to make adjustments

**Step 5: Publish**
1. Click "Publish" button (top right)
2. Page is live immediately at your URL
3. No waiting for deployment

**Time Required:** 20-40 minutes for new page

---

### Editing an Existing Page

**Step 1: Find Page**
1. Go to Builder.io → Content
2. Use search or browse list
3. Click page entry

**Step 2: Edit Content**
1. Click "Edit" button
2. Click any component on canvas
3. Update properties in right panel
4. Changes show in real-time

**Step 3: Save & Publish**
1. Changes auto-save as you work
2. Click "Publish" when done

## Managing Contact Form (Jotform)

**What You Need:** Jotform account access

**Access:** https://www.jotform.com/myforms

### Editing Form Fields

**Step 1: Access Form**
1. Log into Jotform
2. Click "My Forms"
3. Find Fern & Fog contact form
4. Click "Edit Form"

**Step 2: Add/Edit/Remove Fields**

**To Add a Field:**
1. Drag field type from left panel (Text, Email, Dropdown, etc.)
2. Drop onto form
3. Click field to configure
4. Set label, placeholder, validation
5. Mark as required (if needed)

**To Edit a Field:**
1. Click field on form
2. Update properties in right panel
3. Change label, help text, options

**To Remove a Field:**
1. Click field
2. Click trash icon
3. Confirm deletion

**Step 3: Save**
1. Click "Publish" button (top right)
2. Changes are live immediately

---

### Configuring Email Notifications

**Step 1: Access Settings**
1. Edit form in Jotform
2. Click "Settings" tab (top)
3. Click "Emails" in left sidebar

**Step 2: Configure Notification Email**

**To You (Admin Notification):**
1. Click "Notification Emails"
2. Enter your email address
3. Customize subject line (optional)
4. Choose fields to include
5. Save

**To User (Auto-Responder):**
1. Click "Autoresponder Email"
2. Enter sender name (e.g., "Fern & Fog Creations")
3. Write thank you message
4. Save

**Email Types:**
- **Notification:** Alerts you of new submission
- **Autoresponder:** Confirms submission to user
- **Conditional:** Sends based on answers (advanced)

## When to Contact a Developer

You'll need developer assistance for these tasks:

### Content Changes Requiring Code

❌ **Gallery Content**
- **What:** Update gallery items, stories, or images
- **Why:** Gallery uses hardcoded data file
- **File:** `data/gallery.ts`

❌ **About Page (`/about`)**
- **What:** Edit existing about page content
- **Why:** Page is hardcoded React component
- **File:** `app/about/page.tsx`
- **Alternative:** Create new about page at `/about-us` in Builder.io

❌ **Category Definitions**
- **What:** Add/edit/remove product categories
- **Why:** Categories stored in local data file
- **File:** `data/products.ts`
- **Note:** Can manually enter in CategoryGridBlock, but not synced

❌ **Navigation Menus**
- **What:** Update header or footer links
- **Why:** Currently using hardcoded fallback
- **File:** `lib/builder/navigation.ts`
- **Future:** Can be moved to Builder.io (needs developer setup)

❌ **Policy Pages**
- **What:** Update shipping, returns, privacy, or terms policies
- **Why:** Pages are hardcoded
- **Files:** `app/policies/[policy]/page.tsx`

❌ **Contact Page Layout**
- **What:** Change contact page design or content around form
- **Why:** Page template is hardcoded
- **File:** `app/contact/page.tsx`
- **Note:** Form fields are editable in Jotform

❌ **Product Page Templates**
- **What:** Change layout/design of product detail pages
- **Why:** Templates are hardcoded React components
- **Files:** `app/product/[handle]/page.tsx`

### Technical Setup & Configuration

❌ **New Custom Component for Builder.io**
- **What:** Create new reusable component type
- **Why:** Requires React development and registration
- **Example:** Testimonials slider, Instagram feed, etc.

❌ **Connect Builder.io to Shopify**

❌ **Set Up Builder.io Navigation**
- **What:** Enable editable navigation in Builder.io

❌ **Add New Payment Methods**

❌ **Custom Functionality**

- **Why:** Requires custom development

### Analytics & Tracking

- **What:** Track website visitors and conversions
- **Why:** Requires adding tracking codes to site

❌ **Set Up Facebook Pixel**
- **What:** Track conversions for Facebook ads
- **Why:** Requires adding pixel code

## Tips for Success

### Working with Images

**Before Uploading:**
1. **Resize:** Use correct dimensions for component
2. **Compress:** Aim for under 500KB per image
3. **Format:** Use JPG for photos, PNG for graphics
4. **Name:** Use descriptive names (e.g., `sea-glass-earrings-blue.jpg`)

**Free Tools:**
- **Compression:** https://tinypng.com/
- **Resizing:** https://squoosh.app/
- **Editing:** Canva, Photoshop, GIMP

### Writing Product Descriptions

**Include:**
- Materials used (be specific)
- Size/dimensions
- Inspiration or story
- Care instructions
- What makes it unique

**Format:**
- Start with short hook sentence
- Use bullet points for details
- End with care instructions

**Example:**
```
Ocean-tumbled blue sea glass set in sterling silver.

• Materials: Authentic sea glass, .925 sterling silver
• Size: 1" drop length
• Found along Cape Cod shores
• Each piece is naturally unique
• Hypoallergenic

Care: Remove before swimming or showering.
Store in provided pouch.
```

### Organizing Products

**Use Collections:**
- By type: Earrings, Necklaces, Wall Art
- By material: Sea Glass, Resin, Driftwood
- By season: Summer Collection, Holiday Gifts
- By price: Under $50, Gift Ideas
- By status: Best Sellers, New Arrivals

**Use Tags Consistently:**
- Material tags: `sea-glass`, `sterling-silver`, `resin`
- Color tags: `blue`, `green`, `amber`
- Style tags: `coastal`, `minimalist`, `statement`
- Use lowercase with hyphens

### Maintaining Brand Consistency

**Voice & Tone:**
- Warm and authentic
- Tell stories about materials and process
- Use coastal/nature language
- Personal but professional

**Visual Style:**
- Use brand colors (moss, fern, parchment, bark, mist, gold)
- Natural, coastal imagery
- Clean product photography
- Consistent filter/editing style

**Photography:**
- Natural light preferred
- White or beach/nature backgrounds
- Show scale with reference objects
- Multiple angles for each product

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long do changes take to appear?

- **Shopify product changes:** 1-2 minutes (cache revalidation)
- **Builder.io page changes:** Instant (real-time)
- **Jotform changes:** Instant (real-time)
- **Code changes:** 2-5 minutes (requires deployment)

### Can I schedule content to publish later?

- **Builder.io:** Yes, use scheduling feature
- **Shopify:** No, but can use draft status
- **Jotform:** No scheduling available

### What happens if I make a mistake?

- **Builder.io:** Has revision history, can revert
- **Shopify:** Can undo or re-edit anytime
- **Jotform:** Can delete/edit submissions
- **Code changes:** Developer can revert via Git

### Can I see who made changes?

- **Builder.io:** Yes, tracks users and changes
- **Shopify:** Yes, activity log shows edits
- **Jotform:** Yes, submission timestamps

### How do I test changes before publishing?

- **Builder.io:** Use "Preview" button
- **Shopify:** Use draft status, preview in theme customizer
- **Jotform:** Use "Preview Form" button

### Can multiple people edit at once?

- **Builder.io:** Yes, but be careful of conflicts
- **Shopify:** Yes, with caution
- **Jotform:** Yes
- **Best Practice:** Communicate with team before editing
